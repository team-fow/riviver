Revive a polluted river!

A hand-drawn educational game designed to teach kids how to recognize — and deal with — water pollution.

Kids progress through a series of levels, which become gradually more challenging — keeping them engaged without being overwhelming.

In each level, they play minigames that teach core concepts from the NGSS.

The world map becomes more lush as they complete levels, demonstrating their impact on the environment.
