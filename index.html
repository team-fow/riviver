Revive a polluted river!

A hand-drawn educational game designed to teach kids how to recognize — and deal with — water pollution.

Progress through a series of levels, which become gradually more challenging — keeping kids engaged without being overwhelming.

Play minigames that teach core concepts from the NGSS — sorting trash, filtering water, and more.

Watch the world map transform, demonstrating kids' impact on the environment. Defeat the evil Polluto!
